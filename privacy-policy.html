Privacy & Policy

Privacy and Policy for MasevTech Solutions

  1. Home
  2. Policy

1. Introduction

MasevTech Solutions (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting the privacy of clients and users of our ERP software, device networking and installation services, and technical support offerings. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you engage with our services.

2. Information We Collect

We gather the following categories of information:

  • - Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address.
  • - Account and Service Data: Username, password (hashed), service preferences, purchase history, support tickets.
  • - Device and Network Information: IP address, device identifiers, network configuration details, system logs.
  • - Usage Metrics: How you interact with our applications, support portal, and other digital tools.
  • - Payment Details: Billing name, payment method (e.g., credit card), transaction records.

    • 3. How We Use Your Information

    Your information is used to:

    1. - Deliver, maintain, and improve our ERP software, networking, and support services.
    2. - Authenticate and manage user accounts.
    3. - Respond to support inquiries and troubleshoot technical issues.
    4. - Process invoices, payments, and refunds.
    5. - Communicate updates, security alerts, and marketing messages (with your consent).
    6. - Analyze service usage to optimize performance and user experience.

    4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

    We may share your information with:
    1. - Service Providers:Third parties who perform functions on our behalf, such as payment processors, hosting providers, and analytics partners.
    2. - Legal and Regulatory Authorities: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and property.
    3. - Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to confidentiality obligations
    NOTE:We do not sell your personal data to marketers or other unaffiliated third parties.

    5. Data Security

    We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, and misuse. These measures include:
    1. - data transmission (TLS/SSL).
    2. - Secure network architecture and firewalls.
    3. - Role-based access controls and strong password policies.
    4. - Regular vulnerability assessments and penetration testing.
    Note: While we strive to protect your information, no system is completely immune to security breaches.

    6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

    Our website and applications may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:
    1. - Remember your preferences and login status.
    2. - Analyze site traffic and usage patterns.
    3. - Serve personalized content and advertisements.
    Note:You can manage cookie settings through your browser preferences or opt out of analytics tracking where applicable.

    7. Data Retention

    We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to:
    1. - Fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy.
    2. - Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.
    3. - Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements.
    Note:After the retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

    8. Your Privacy Rights

    Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
    • - Access and obtain a copy of your personal data.
    • - Correct or update inaccurate information.
    • - Request deletion or restriction of processing.
    • - Object to certain processing activities.
    • - Port your data to another service provider.
    Note: To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

    9. International Data Transfers

    If we transfer your data outside your country of residence, we will ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses or other approved mechanisms. ---

    10. Updates to This Policy

    If we transfer your data outside your country of residence, we will ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses or other approved mechanisms.

    Note:We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated version on our website and updating the “Effective Date.” Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
